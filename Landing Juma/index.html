Our Story

"Connect passionate people and build real‐world projects together."

The seed for Jumadevs was planted long ago by Junior, who, as he was learning to code, wished he had a friend by his side—someone to share questions, challenges, and small victories. Even though he didn’t know anyone else into programming, he held onto the dream of building a community where people make friends and learn together.

That idea came to life during high school, when Junior and Marili teamed up for DGTI’s “Prototipos y Proyecto de Emprendimiento” contest. That spark showed them the power of collaboration, and they decided to turn their prototype into a real platform.

Today we’re still in beta—our community is growing, the first coding challenges are underway, and we invite you to join us from day one. Sign up for the waitlist and help shape the future of collaborative learning.
